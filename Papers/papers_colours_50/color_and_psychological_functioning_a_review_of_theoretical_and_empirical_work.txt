color and psychological functioning: a review of theoretical and empirical work

this review examines the relationship between color and psychological functioning, highlighting how different colors can influence mood, cognition, and behavior. the authors discuss theories and empirical studies that show specific effects of colors such as red, blue, and green on various cognitive and emotional tasks.

Keywords: color psychology, mood, cognition, behavior, theoretical review, empirical studies, red color effects, blue color effects, green color effects
